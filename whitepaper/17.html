Assetization & Publishing Engine
The crux of Hands value is our ability to ‘assetize’ or create a digital
representation on the Ethereum blockchain. These representations
are referred to as ’Namespace Tokens’, as their publicly accessible
name attribute is composed of a logical grouping of unique
identifiers. ERC20-standard ’Namespace Tokens’ are created via
smart contract, each with an identifying payload that notarizes the
user and its transaction. As we consider the shift towards ERC223
tokens, namespace tokens will also be compliant with this new
protocol.

Hands Assetization
The crux of Hands’s value is our ability to ”assetize” or create a digital rep-
resentation of transaction on the Ethereum blockchain. These representations are
referred to as ’Namespace Tokens’, as their publicly accessible name attribute is
composed of a logical grouping of unique identifiers.
